# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Researchers, students, analysts, and privacy-conscious knowledge workers who want to ask questions of their own documents without uploading those documents to a third-party service. They visit the site to understand the product quickly, confirm platform support, and download the desktop app.

## Product Purpose

AI Notebook is a free, open-source, local-first alternative to NotebookLM for macOS and Windows. The website should make the private, offline workflow immediately tangible, build trust through specific product evidence, and lead visitors to the latest GitHub release.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and quietly confident. The experience should feel native, considered, and trustworthy, with the simplicity and product focus associated with Apple's marketing pages while retaining AI Notebook's own blue-violet identity.

## Anti-references

Do not look like the README was converted into stacked web sections. Do not resemble a generic AI startup template, a neon developer tool, or a dense SaaS dashboard. Avoid feature-list storytelling, endless identical cards, gradient headlines, decorative glass panels, vague claims, and aggressive sales language.

## Design Principles

- Show the real product before explaining it.
- Build a visual product narrative instead of repeating repository documentation.
- Make privacy concrete, not performative.
- Use restraint to focus attention on the download path.
- Let native desktop details communicate quality.
- Keep open-source trust visible throughout the page.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve clear keyboard focus, semantic structure, sufficient contrast, useful alternative text, responsive layouts, and a complete reduced-motion experience.
